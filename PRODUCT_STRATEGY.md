You currently have:

**App 1: Meal Planning**

- Weekly planner

- Grocery list

- Pantry

- Meal prep

**App 2: Recipe Discovery**

- Search recipes

- Browse cuisines

- Save favorites

**App 3: Restaurant Discovery**

- AI Map Explorer

- Restaurant reviews

- Restaurant search

**App 4: Recipe Generation**

- Restaurant dish → home recipe

- AI recipe creation

Those are each strong ideas individually, but together they can feel unfocused.

If I were using it, I'd ask:

> "Is this a meal planner?"
> "A recipe app?"
> "A Yelp/Google Maps competitor?"
> "An AI cooking assistant?"

The strongest and most unique idea may actually be:

### Option A: Restaurant-to-Recipe AI

**Find a dish anywhere → recreate it at home**

This is much more differentiated than another meal planner.

Or:

### Option B: AI Kitchen Assistant

Start with:

- Pantry

- Recipe generation

- Grocery list

Remove:

- Weekly planner

- Restaurant discovery

Much simpler.

Or:

### Option C: Food Discovery Platform

Focus on:

- AI Map Explorer

- Restaurant recommendations

- Dish exploration

- Recipe recreation

Almost like "Google Maps + Pinterest + AI Chef."
